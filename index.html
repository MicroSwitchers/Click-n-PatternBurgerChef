How to Play:

  • Double Tap: Bun 🍞
  • Single Tap: Patty 🥩
  • Long Tap: Lettuce 🥬
  • Triple Tap: Cheese 🧀
  • Single Tap + Long Tap: Tomato 🍅
  • Two Long Taps: Onion 🧅
  • Two Short Taps + Long Tap: Pickle 🥒

Game Rules

  • You have 3 strikes. Make mistakes and your game will be affected.
  • 1 Strike: Restart the current burger.
  • 2 Strikes: Restart the current level.
  • 3 Strikes: Restart the entire game.
  • Complete all orders in a level to advance to the next one.
  • Use the correct tap gestures for each ingredient as shown in the instructions.
  • The game objective is accumulating the highest score to beat your personal best.
